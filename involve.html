Get Involved

There are a number of valuable ways to get involved with Yosemite.

Work With Us

The National Park Service and several other organizations have employment opportunities.

Volunteer

Yosemite has a large and vibrant volunteer program, welcoming people from all over the world into a variety of positions.

Donate

Make donations to these Yosemite's non-profit partners: Yosemite Conservancy, NatureBridge, and Friends of Yosemite Search and Rescue.
